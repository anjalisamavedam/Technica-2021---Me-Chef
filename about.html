About Me Chef

Anjali Samavedam & Beatrice Chung are prospective computer science majors at the University of Maryland College Park. Although we are excited to jumpstart our careers in technology, we are apprehensive of dorm food and potential unhealthy eating habits that we may develop in our university years. Additionally, eating out is not sustainable with our non-existent bank accounts :( Thus, we created Me Chef.

Me Chef allows busy college students and other individuals a convenient method of retrieving delicious meals. With many preference categories included, individuals can select vegetarian, pescatarian, low calorie, and many more options to find optimal meal recipes. We hope to encourage healthy eating in our local community coupled with delicious recipes.
